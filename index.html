Physics Formulas- rj

  • Force (F): F = m × a, where m is mass and a is acceleration.
  • Acceleration (a): a = F / m, where F is force and m is mass.
  • Momentum (p): p = m × v, where m is mass and v is velocity.
  • Kinetic Energy (KE): KE = 0.5 × m × v²
  • Potential Energy (PE): PE = m × g × h, where g is acceleration due to gravity and h is height.
